# ShopKart Revenue & Retention Analysis (Power BI)

## Project Objective
This project analyzes revenue performance, customer retention, return rate, and category contribution to identify key business insights and improvement opportunities.

## Tools & Skills Used
- Power BI  
- DAX  
- Data Modeling  
- KPI Design  
- Business Insight Generation  

## Key KPIs
- **Total Revenue:** 2.20bn  
- **Repeat Revenue:** 91%  
- **Return Rate:** 7.9%  
- **Revenue Leakage:** 16%  
- **Customer Count:** 10K  
- **Average Order Value:** 44K  

## Analysis Performed
- Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis  
- Revenue by Category Breakdown  
- Return Rate by Category  
- Customer Type Revenue Split (Repeat vs One-time)  
- Revenue vs Target Performance  
- Segment-Level Filtering (Business, Prime, Regular)  

## Key Insights
- Business revenue is heavily dependent on the Electronics category.  
- 91% of total revenue comes from repeat customers, indicating strong retention.  
- Revenue shows a declining trend in recent months and requires investigation.  
- Return rate is relatively balanced across categories.  

## Business Recommendations
- Reduce dependency on a single category by promoting diversified product growth.  
- Investigate reasons behind declining revenue trends (volume, pricing, customer drop).  
- Improve new customer acquisition to balance repeat revenue dominance.  
- Monitor return rate to reduce revenue leakage and protect margins.
